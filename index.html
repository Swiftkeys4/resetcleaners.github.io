Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the instant quote?

Our instant quote calculator provides a reliable estimate based on your selections. During our consultation, we'll confirm the final pricing and discuss any specific needs or preferences you may have. Most quotes are within $10-20 of the final price.

What if I need to reschedule?

Life happens! You can easily reschedule or cancel through your booking confirmation email, or simply contact us directly at least 24 hours in advance. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your busy lifestyle without any fees.

Do you provide all cleaning supplies?

Absolutely! We bring all professional-grade supplies and equipment needed for your service. If you have specific product preferences, allergies, or eco-friendly requirements, just let us know and we'll accommodate your needs at no extra charge.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Reset Cleaners LLC is fully licensed, bonded, and insured for your complete protection. Your property and belongings are covered, and all our team members are background-checked professionals.

How do I pay for services?

We accept all major payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, cash, Venmo, and PayPal. Payment can be made securely online when booking or at the time of service completion—whatever works best for you.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Wenatchee, East Wenatchee, Cashmere, Leavenworth, and surrounding valley communities. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location—we're always expanding our reach!

Do you offer recurring services?

Yes! We offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly recurring cleaning services with discounted rates. Set up your schedule once and enjoy the consistency of a professionally maintained home without the hassle of rebooking.

What if I'm not satisfied?

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely happy with our service, contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to address any concerns at no additional cost. We stand behind our work 100%.
